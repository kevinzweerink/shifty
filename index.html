Shifty

This program was used to generate letterforms by tracking the direction of the user's gaze as they traced an imagined letterform on a blank surface.

The program tracks a specific range of greens as they appear in the webcam video. Try the headband on to test

Once the program begins to track the headband, the following key commands may be used:

  • SpaceBegins recording, draws a dot each frame at the centroid of the detected area
  • DClears the current drawing
